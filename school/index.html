About Us

A School should be a place of Light, of Liberty and of Learning

The true purpose of education is to make minds, not careers. We become what we think ourselves to be. Thought and action go hand in hand at the LJP society. The schools of LJP are an indispensable part of the education fabric. Mr Praveen Gupta has been a stimulating member of the LJP fraternity

His motivating and enriching words inspire everyone to work towards triumph. He ignites the spark in us which makes us believe that in order to succeed, we must first believe that we can.


Director Message


            
Mr Praveen Gupta


Our focus is to develop our students as global citizens, with tolerance, respect and appreciation of diverse cultures and religions for a life-time learning experience. They should be self-motivated, independent, confident decision makers to take up a leadership roles in future. we arm our students with technological supremacy and help them integrate it with values, morals and our cultural legacy. The school curriculum has been painstakingly planned on scientific guidelines to provide students with intellectual stimulation, physical robustness, social adaptability, emotional independence and leadership qualities. It is our endeavor to establish a quality I wish that LJP Public School, Meerut with its foresightedness, firm determination and hard work will successfully strengthen the community’s sense of identity and enhance the pride and prestige of this institution. With the positive winds of economic upswing, coupled with a stage where the middle class and society is going nuclear in size and the household is being served with more than one source of income, aspirations have remarkably risen in the quality of education that young couples wish to give to their wards
